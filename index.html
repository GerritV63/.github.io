Off-market and personal contact

Our hotel portfolio is not published

Highlight Hotel Portfolio works off-market.
HHP doesn't publicize the hotels for sale. 
Nor does HHP send sales information to long lists of customers.

At first our customers receive general information of the property.
Depending on the situation the name and address of the property and the name of the sellers will be kept confidential until a NDA and LOI is signed.

Our buyers will be contacted individually to introduce new hotels for sale

HHP holds a list of interested buyers / investors.
HHP contacts potential buyers personally.
We know what our customers are looking for and we search for suited hotels and hotel projects. 
Only the types of hotels of their interest will be passed on to the investor.

Discrete

Not only the seller's name and contact data will be held back at first. Also the name and address of the property will be held back as well.
First the buyer explicitily has to express his interest in the given data of the property. Than the needed documents will be made up (LOI / NDA / Financial statement / Due Diligence). After that, a meeting will be scheduled between all parties.

Flexible negotiations
